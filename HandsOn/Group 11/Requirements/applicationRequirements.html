Application requirements

Objectives of the application

  • First objetive:

    Representation of pollution levels in the different areas of Madrid, with the graphics of each particle.

  • Second objetive:

    Web page in real time to notify the population of the level of concentration and to take into account their different risks. Provide relevant information to institutions to take measures if necessary and that there are risks to human health.

  • Third objetive:

    Classification of the most harmful elements for health
