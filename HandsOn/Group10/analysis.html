Resource Naming Strategy

- Domain: http://www.madridcultural.es/cultural-activities/

Ontology

- Path: http://www.madridcultural.es/cultural-activities#
- Pattern: http://www.madridcultural.es/cultural-activities#«entity_name»
- Example: http://www.madridcultural.es/cultural-activities#IFEMA

Individuals

- Path: http://www.madridcultural.es/cultural-activities/resource/
- Pattern: http://www.madridcultural.es/cultural-activities/resource/«resource_class»/«resource_name»
- Example: http://www.madridcultural.es/cultural-activities/resource/Camps/actividadPrueba
