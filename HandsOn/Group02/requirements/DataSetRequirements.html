Traffic Accidents of the City of Madrid

Traffic accidents in the City of Madrid registered by the Municipal Police. This relationship refers to traffic accidents with victims and / or damage to assets.

Requirement 1

Data is from open data portal of the Madrid City Council

Madrid

Requirement 2

Data is available as a CSV file.

Requirement 3

Data has an open license.

View License

Requirement 4

In data base we can find some variables that can be linked with real world entities, such as: Distrito, lugar del accidente, tipo de persona y tipo de vehículo.

Requirement 5

In the web page "Portal de datos abiertos del Ayuntamiento de Madrid" we can find some aditional documentation about the dataset, such as: the structure of the dataset, metadata and so on.
