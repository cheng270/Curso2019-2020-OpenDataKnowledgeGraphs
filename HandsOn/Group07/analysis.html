Resource Naming Strategy

The resource naming strategy defined for the data follows the best practices and tips provided by the Cool URIs guide [Sauermann and Cyganiak, 2008].

According to the principles stated above, hash URIs will be used for our ontology since it is rather small.

According to the same good practices, slash URIs will be used for our resource as it is a relatively large dataset. Furthermore, thinking about the application and the source of these data, their quantity could grow considerably in a short time, as they are detected by sensors. The content negotiation chosen for the 303 redirection due to the slash URIs is the forwarding to a generic document, since we expect the different representations to transmit similar information in different forms (as advised by the already mentioned Cool URIs).

The URI domain chosen is http://www.airqualitymadrid.es. We opted for this type of URI after checking that it was free. In this way we can assume to have direct control.

  • Ontology model classes:
    following the tips, have the form of http://www.airqualitymadrid.es/ontologies/<ontologyName>#<ClassName>

  • Ontology model properties:
    the classes in the ontology will have the form of http://www.airqualitymadrid.es/ontologies/<ontologyName>#<propertyName>

  • Instances:
    the instances in the ontology will have the form of http://www.airqualitymadrid.es/resource/<className>/<identifier>
